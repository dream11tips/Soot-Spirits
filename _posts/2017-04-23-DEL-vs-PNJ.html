DD vs KXIP Match Preview

Delhi, April 23 at 08:00 PM IST

Delhi Daredevils host in-form Kings XI Punjab for their first home fixture of the season. The two teams have had dissimilar results thus far, with Kings XI sitting at the top of the points table while the Daredevils are reeling at the bottom.

DD have won only once in 5 games so far, but they would be hoping for the change in fortune at their home ground. In their last game against Royal Challengers Bangalore, they went down by 6 wickets.

On the other hand, KXIP have emerged as one of the contenders of IPL 2018, having won four out of five games so far in the competition. In their previous game, they destroyed Kolkata Knight Riders in their backyard.
